## Mark Johnson - Rory's Story Cubes: An analog storytelling game engine

<!--more-->

This paper explores how Rory's Story Cubes can be used as a flexible, low-prep storytelling tool for language learning. It highlights how playful prompts can support creativity, collaboration, and classroom discussion.
