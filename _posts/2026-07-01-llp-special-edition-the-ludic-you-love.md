# ❤️ The Ludic You Love

Welcome to the special issue **The Ludic You Love**.

This collection brings together educators, researchers, and practitioners who share games, activities, texts, and playful experiences that have made a lasting impact on their teaching and learning. From board games and storytelling tools to digital games and creative classroom practices, each contribution explores a particular ludic object and reflects on its educational potential.

Together, these papers offer a snapshot of the diverse ways play continues to inspire language learning communities around the world.
