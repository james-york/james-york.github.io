## Zachary Lubbers - Around the World: A Basketball-Inspired Language Game

<!--more-->

This paper explores how a basketball-inspired game can be adapted for language learning through competition and quick retrieval practice. It shows how familiar sports formats can create engaging opportunities for speaking and vocabulary review.
