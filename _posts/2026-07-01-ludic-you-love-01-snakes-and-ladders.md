## Jonathan deHaan - Snakes and Ladders: Roll, Reflect, Remix!

<!--more-->

This paper explores how a familiar board game can be reimagined for reflection, remixing, and language learning. It offers a playful way to connect game mechanics with classroom discussion and creative adaptation.
