## KEY POINTS

- **What is this?** This paper describes an online escape-the-room game that helps TEFL students check their linguistic expertise in a fun and engaging way.
- **Why did you make it?** Linguistics can be a demanding and difficult subject for students of TEFL. I created the game to offer my students a motivating and fun way to deepen their understanding of basic linguistic terminology and methods.
- **Who is it for?** This is primarily geared towards students of English linguistics but is also a fun activity for anyone interested in the topic.
