## Trần Lê Khang (Ken) - Teaching Financial Literacy Using EZWorld

<!--more-->

This paper explores how EZWorld can be used to teach financial literacy through engaging game-based activities. It highlights how play can support practical vocabulary, decision-making, and learner participation.
