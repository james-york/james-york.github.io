## Trần Lê Khang (Ken) - Teaching Nouns Using Things in Rings and the Concept of Venn Diagram

<!--more-->

This paper explores how Things in Rings and the concept of the Venn diagram can be used to teach nouns in a creative and accessible way. It highlights how visual organization and playful categorization support language learning and classroom discussion.
