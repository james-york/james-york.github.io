## Erik X. Raj, Ava D. Charleston, & Noelle Parrino - Wonder Flower: Planting Seeds of Life and Language in a Pixelated City

<!--more-->

This paper explores how a pixelated city setting can support language learning, creativity, and care for place through the metaphor of planting seeds.
