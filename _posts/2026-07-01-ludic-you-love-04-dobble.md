## Monika van der Velden - Seeing words differently: using Dobble for vocabulary learning

<!--more-->

This paper explores how Dobble can be used to support vocabulary learning by encouraging quick visual recognition, discussion, and meaningful language use. It highlights how a simple card game can be adapted for classroom practice and learner engagement.
