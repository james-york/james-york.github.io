## Diane Neubauer and Reed Riggs - Story-asking: Imaginative, Co-created Stories

<!--more-->

This paper explores how story-asking can be used to create imaginative and co-created storytelling experiences in language learning. It highlights how shared prompts and collaborative meaning-making support learner engagement and creativity.
