## Martin Sedaghat - Picture This: Wordless Picturebooks in the Language Classroom

<!--more-->

This paper explores how wordless picturebooks can be used to support language learning through observation, inference, storytelling, and discussion. It highlights how visual literacy and imagination can work together in the classroom.
